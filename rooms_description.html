Single Room

Our comfortable individual standard rooms provide rest, relaxation and privacy if you want a room to yourself, whether travelling alone or with a group. Located on floors 1-9, these rooms offer inland views of the surrounding hills and town which are especially lovely in the mornings or when Magaluf lights up at night. Each room has a private terrace with chairs and a small table, and they are fully equipped with an en-suite bathroom, mini-fridge and flat-screen TV. A safe and other extras are available to hire separately at reception.

Double Room

After a long day exploring the area or an equally long night enjoying local bars and restaurants, our comfortable standard rooms provide rest and relaxation. Located on floors 1-9, these rooms offer a variety of views including inland vistas of the surrounding hills and town, or overlooking our terrace areas with pools. Each room has a private terrace with chairs and a small table, and they are fully equipped with an en-suite bathroom, mini-fridge and flat-screen TV. A safe and other extras are available to hire separately at reception.

Suite Room

A larger room (24m²) with direct access to 'La Terracita', our first-floor sun deck with plunge pool, bar and Balinese beds (NEW for 2019). The room has a stylish private terrace (4m²) with views of 'La Terracita' plus two sun loungers and a small table - ideal for relaxing in the sun after a dip in the nearby pool. It is fully equipped with an en-suite bathroom, mini-fridge and seating area with small sofa, plus premium touches such as a Nespresso machine.
